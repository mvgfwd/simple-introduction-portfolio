ABOUT ME

Firstly first, I'm sorry because this is my first portfolio and it's super simple like its Title. Using pure HTML and little bit JavaScript and Bootstrap.

So, I'm Yonathan Simbolon, born and raised in Batam, Indonesia. My love for computers started at the age of 12 when my parents introduced me to this fascinating technology. From that point, I was determined to become a technology expert and learn everything there was to know about computers. However, my hunger for knowledge was so strong that I decided to join the aviation industry as a technician at the age of 17, seeking new experiences and knowledge. Working in the aviation industry for three years provided me with a wealth of experience and a great opportunity to learn. Despite my job as a technician, I never stopped learning about computers on my own, practicing various programming languages and technology frameworks.

In the future, I hope to collaborate with startups, IT departments, or any team as a developer or programmer to achieve my childhood dream. I am open to opportunities to learn software engineering and programming, and I am excited to build my skills as a programmer. With computer and programming, I am happy because I can create something from scratch and constantly learn new things. Currently, I am diving into web development and have gained experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, ReactJS, NextJS, Redux, Router, and more. I am also learning Typescript and plan to use the Angular framework soon. I have worked on various projects, from building simple websites to cloning big apps like Amazon and Facebook. I am eager to expand my knowledge and work with a team to develop exciting projects and become a great programmer. Now, I am studying computer science at Binus University to expand my knowledge and skills.
